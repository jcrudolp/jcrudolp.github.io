About ChromaCorrect

ChromaCorrect is a Google Chrome extension which changes the colors of the text, text background, and hyperlinks. This extension can be enabled on any webpage and will change the colors of it to have more contrast and be able to see. All colors have been carefully chosen to provide strong contrast between text, background and hyperlinks. Additionally, all of the colors have been chosen from a colorblind-friendly color palette. This will allow colorblind users to easily see what has and has not been viewed, which was a big complaint from colorblind users. The usability is very simple too! All you have to do is download the extension and enable it in the toolbar. It should immediately change the colors of the page.
